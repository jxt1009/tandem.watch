# Tandem.watch Icon Design

## Concept
The icon represents synchronized watch parties - two people watching together in tandem.

## Color Scheme (Modern & Tech-Forward)
- **Primary**: Deep Purple/Violet (#7C3AED) - Trust, sync, connection
- **Secondary**: Bright Cyan (#06B6D4) - Real-time, energy, tech
- **Accent**: Coral/Pink (#FF6B6B) - Human connection, warmth
- **Background**: Dark slate (#1E293B) - Netflix-like dark theme

## Icon Elements
- Two overlapping play buttons in tandem/synchronized position
- Connection/sync lines between them
- Modern, minimalist design
- Works well at 16x16, 48x48, 128x128 sizes
